YouTube enforces policies to maintain user safety and prevent harm.

Key Restricted Categories:
1. Violence
   - Graphic footage, violent injury, depictions of death, or encouragement of violence are prohibited.
   - Threats or incitement toward individuals or groups are not allowed.

2. Harmful or Dangerous Behavior
   - Encouraging dangerous physical acts or self-harm is prohibited.
   - Content promoting illegal activities such as drug misuse or weapon misuse is restricted.

3. Misinformation
   - Medical misinformation that contradicts authoritative health sources is prohibited.
   - False claims about public safety, elections, or government processes may be removed.

4. Hate Speech
   - Content promoting hatred toward protected characteristics (race, gender, caste, religion, etc.) is not allowed.

5. Child Safety
   - Content involving minors must avoid sexualization, harm, or endangerment.
   - Targeted harassment or bullying of minors is prohibited.

6. Regulated Goods
   - Promotion or sale of weapons, drugs, or age-restricted products requires compliance with strict guidelines.

7. Sponsored Content
   - Paid promotions must be disclosed clearly.
   - Misleading ads or affiliate links violate policy.

YouTube may demonetize videos for:
- Excessive profanity
- Controversial topics
- Dangerous acts
- Unsuitable content for advertisers
